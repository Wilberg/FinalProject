About the app

An introduction and description

Through advanced algorithms the user will be presented with other students of similar interests, based on the prefered distance. Its goal is to help students find new friends based on their hobbies and interests, rather than the looks. You will have access to various components, such as private chats, posts and profiles. You will be given a chance to customize your profile, however none of these customizations are required. To use the app, Koality only needs basic information to match other students, including your age, school, and username. Using psychological factors such as colors, icons and minimalism, Koality promises a neat user experience.

Private Chat

This is the Koality Prototype on the posts page

Koality does not give you the ability to add a user as a friend. However, through a simple Private Chat component, you are able to keep contact with other users at any time. You can send a private message by entering a users profile and hitting the “Send a message” button.

Posts

Anyone can share their thoughts by publishing a post. It can be anything on your mind, and will be reached by similarly minded people near you. You can also browse other post, and reply to given post if desired. A user's most recent posts will show up on their profile. In that way, you can get a better insight into someone’s personality by seeing what they post.

Anonymity

The main difference between Koality and mainstream media like Facebook, Instagram and Twitter, is the anonymity. While using Koality, we can ensure you stay anonymous. This way, we give you more freedom to be who you actually are, hence get to know like minded people.
