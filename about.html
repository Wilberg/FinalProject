About the app

An introduction and description

What is Koality

Koality is an app designed to bring students together. Through advanced algorithms the user will be presented with other students of similar interests, based on the prefered distance. Its goal is to help students find new friends based on their hobbies and interests, rather than the looks. The user will have access to various components, such as private chats, posts and profiles. The main difference between Koality and mainstream media like Facebook, Instagram and Twitter, is the anonymity. By using Koality, we can ensure each user to stay anonymous. Koality gives the user a chance to customize their profile, however none of these customizations are required. To use the app, Koality only needs basic information to match other students, including age, school, and username.
