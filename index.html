Koality is a great place to meet other students! It is a social network that makes it easy to meet other students with the same interests as you! Your new social circle starts here!

The problem

According to a new report, almost 1 of 4 students miss someone to socialize with on a daily basis. Most students have to relocate in order to take up their studies, and must break up with their old frieds. They have problems with developing a new network, making the students feel alone and isolated.

Read more

Our solution

Our solution will help students find people to socialize with. A significant number of students find it difficult to find new friends in real life. By using this app, students will be able to make contact with other peers just by a few clicks on their phone. Our target group find it easier to reach out through an online platform. Hopefully, this will contribute to more students finding friends in an educational and socially stimulating community.
