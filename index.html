Grow your social circle, student!

Koality is the best place to meet other students! It is a social network that makes it easy for you to just be your self and to meet like-minded people. Your new social circle starts here!

The problem

According to the annual SHoT (Studentenes Helse- og Trivselsundersøkelse) Report of 2018, 23% of students admit that they miss someone to be with on a daily basis. Many students move because of their studies and has problems fitting in with the environment. This often leaves the student feeling alone and isolated.

Read more

Our solution

Our app will help students find people to socialize with. A significant amount of students find it difficult to reach out to others in real life. With our app, students will be able to contact others more easily, online. Most people find it easier to reach out through the safety of a screen, like a mobile device. Hopefully, this will contribute to more students finding themselves in an educational and socially stimulating community.
