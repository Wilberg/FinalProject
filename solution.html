Our Solution

The solution is here

Our solution to the problem of loneliness among students, is a social network app named Koality. The goal is to help students connect to other students, and to create an educational and supportive community where every student is seen and accepted for who they are.

The target audience

Young and economically weak students are some of the students with the highest rates of loneliness (Knapstad & Heradstveit & Sivertsen, 2018, p. 81). That is why we have created a solution with those groups in mind. By creating a social network app, we can deal with the issue of loneliness in a way that is both familiar to the target audience, and free to use.

According to the Students’ Health and Wellbeing Study, 99.7% of students in Norway use social media to some extent, and young students are the most frequent users of social media (Knapstad et al., 2018, p. 93). This means the vast majority of students, and especially the younger students, already have experience using similar types of applications. This familiarity, in addition to the app being free, means that our solution will be very accessible and relevant for the main target audience.

The goal

A significant amount of students find it difficult to reach out to others in real life. With our app, students will be able to contact others more easily, online. Most people find it easier to reach out through the safety of a screen, like a mobile device. Additionally, everyone is anonymous in the app, something that is intended to make meeting new people easier. Hopefully, this will contribute to more students finding themselves in an educational and socially stimulating community.
